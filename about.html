Our History & Mission

Founded in 1984 by visionaries including Pam Del Duca and Florence "Snake Lady" Nelson, the Scottsdale Char Women was created to foster an environment where high-profile female professionals could connect and thrive. Our name draws from the colloquialism for "someone who gets things done."

From our traditional spring trail rides to our fall reunions, we value the profound connections made among women leaders. We leverage our collective influence and expertise to give back and shape the future of Scottsdale.
